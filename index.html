About the Conference

Digital transformation is revolutionizing industries, reshaping business models, and enhancing operational efficiency. This conference serves as a premier platform for industry leaders, academicians, and professionals to examine the transformative impact of AI, automation, big data, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and digital strategies on modern businesses. Through insightful keynote sessions, expert panel discussions, and interactive engagements, participants will gain strategic perspectives on emerging trends, challenges, and innovative approaches to leveraging digital advancements for sustainable growth and competitive advantage.

about the college

ARC Viswanathan College is a prestigious self-financing institution affiliated to Annamalai University, Chidambaram, and recognized by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Established in 2000, the college has been dedicated to delivering high-quality education, particularly to students from rural and underprivileged backgrounds, for over two decades. Founded under the ARC N. Viswanathan Chettiyar Trust, the institution embodies the philanthropic vision of the ARC family, a distinguished name in the Mayiladuthurai district, renowned for its legacy in the gold and silver jewellery industry. The college offers a comprehensive range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including B.A. (English), B.Sc. (Mathematics, Computer Science) and B.Sc Artificial Inteligence, B.Com. (General, Computer Applications), B.C.A and B.B.A., along with M.Com. In addition to its academic endeavours, the trust has established several educational and community welfare institutions, such as ARC Natesan I.T.I., ARC Kamatchi Matriculation & Higher Secondary School, and ARC Kamatchi Kalyana Mandapam, with all revenues reinvested into developmental initiatives. With a steadfast commitment to academic excellence, intellectual growth, and holistic personality development, ARC Viswanathan College continues to nurture future leaders by equipping students with industry-relevant skills, ethical values, and a strong foundation for professional success and societal contribution.
